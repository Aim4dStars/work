Panorama

Benefits & features

Working with your adviser - you and your adviser can decide which transactions are actioned by the adviser, which by you and which by both of you.

Links to your whole financial system - you can link your BT Cash Hub to up to 5 external bank accounts making it easier to manage your cash flows.

Control and visibility - You can set the payment dates, frequencies and dollar limits on your transactions. An alert will let you know when funds are transferred out of your account and you can see a full transaction history online.
